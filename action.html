Take Action

Write a letter to your local officials

You can state your background and write a letter to your local officials to take action. Be sure to include pieces of evidence in your letter. You can tell them to enact more laws to ensure that the Equal Pay Act is being followed. This is a great way to take action because you can directly contact the people who make the rules and have the power to change them.
